Department Overview

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) department at Sanjivani University stands as a beacon of excellence in computer science education. Established with the vision of creating industry-ready professionals, our department has consistently evolved to meet the dynamic demands of the technology sector.

Our comprehensive 2-year postgraduate program is meticulously designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. We focus on developing both technical expertise and soft skills, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for leadership roles in the IT industry.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry partnerships, we provide an environment that fosters innovation, creativity, and professional growth.

Our Journey

2010 - Department Establishment

MCA Department established with the first batch of 60 students and 8 faculty members.

2015 - Infrastructure Expansion

Added 3 new computer labs with latest technology and expanded library resources.

2018 - Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with 25+ leading IT companies for internships and placements.

2020 - Research Center

Launched dedicated research center focusing on AI, ML, and emerging technologies with industry-standard equipment.

2023 - Excellence Recognition

Received state-level recognition for outstanding placement records and academic excellence.
