About MCA Program

Program Overview

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a 2-year postgraduate program designed to provide comprehensive knowledge in computer science and applications. Our curriculum combines theoretical foundations with practical skills.

"Bridging the gap between academic excellence and industry requirements."
